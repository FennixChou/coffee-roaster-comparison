Summary

Giesen W6A — Best For

  • Specialty coffee small-batch roasting
  • Daily output under 200 kg
  • Compact roasting spaces
  • Single-phase power (230V)
  • Touchscreen control experience
  • Micro-lot testing & recipe development

Probat UG22 — Best For

  • Mid-scale production roasting
  • Daily output 400–800 kg
  • Dedicated roasting facility (10 m²+)
  • Three-phase power (208V)
  • Multi-product roasting (coffee/cocoa/nuts)
  • German engineering & long-term durability
Key Differences at a Glance:
- Throughput: UG22 is 2–4x the W6A (50-100 vs 24 kg/h)
- Volume: UG22 is 4.9x larger
- Weight: UG22 is 1.85x heavier (900 vs 485 kg)
